## TIPS FOR MAXIMISING YOUR GOOGLE BUSINESS PROFILE

1. **Upload photos** — Add a professional headshot, your logo, and a screenshot of a live agent demo. Profiles with photos get 42% more requests for directions and 35% more website clicks.
2. **Respond to every review** — Even a short "Thank you, [name]!" signals to Google that you're active and boosts ranking.
3. **Post weekly** — Google favours active profiles. Use the posts above as a starting rotation.
4. **Enable messaging** — Turn on Google Business chat so prospects can message you directly. Set the auto-reply to something warm and brand-consistent.
5. **Complete every section** — Fill in business hours, service areas, and all attributes. Incomplete profiles rank lower.
6. **Ask early clients for Google reviews** — Even 5 reviews puts you far ahead of competitors with zero. Use your own Google Reviews Agent once it's live.

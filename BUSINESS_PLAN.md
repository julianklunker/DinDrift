## 1. Executive Summary

DinDrift is a solo AI automation consultancy specialising in building bespoke AI agents for small and medium-sized businesses. Every company — regardless of size or industry — has repetitive, time-consuming tasks that should be automated. DinDrift delivers that automation in the form of intelligent agents that integrate into existing workflows, communicate in the company's tone, and operate 24/7 without supervision.

The value proposition is simple: **replace manual busywork with AI agents that work while you sleep**, freeing the business owner and their staff to focus on growth, client relationships, and the work that actually matters.

---

## 2. Problem

Small and medium businesses waste enormous time on:
- Answering the same customer questions repeatedly
- Manual appointment booking and reminders
- Chasing no-shows and rebooking clients
- Sorting and responding to emails
- Requesting and managing Google reviews
- Bookkeeping data entry and expense categorisation

These tasks are not value-creating — they are operational overhead. Yet most SMBs cannot afford enterprise software suites or large development teams. They need lean, targeted solutions that work from day one.

---

## 3. Solution — AI Agents

DinDrift builds custom AI agents that integrate directly into a business's existing tools and communication channels. Each agent is:

- **Purpose-built** — designed for one job, done excellently
- **Brand-aligned** — communicates in the company's tone and language
- **Integrated** — connects to existing calendars, inboxes, CRMs, or booking systems
- **Always on** — operates 24/7 without manual intervention
- **Measurable** — delivers clear metrics (time saved, bookings made, reviews gained)

## 4. Service Catalogue

### 4.1 Secretary Agent *(Administration)*
Handles appointment scheduling, confirmations, and correspondence. Syncs with Google Calendar or Outlook. Responds to enquiries in the business's tone.
**Target clients:** Clinics, consultants, freelancers, service businesses.

### 4.2 Economy Manager Agent *(Finance)*
Automates bookkeeping data entry, expense categorisation, and financial reporting. Integrates with Danish accounting tools (E-conomic, Billy, Dinero).
**Target clients:** Any SMB with monthly bookkeeping overhead.

### 4.3 Website Chatbot *(Customer Service)*
An AI chatbot embedded on the client's website. Answers FAQs 24/7, qualifies leads, and escalates complex queries to a human.
**Target clients:** E-commerce, hospitality, service businesses, clinics.

### 4.4 Email Assistant *(Communication)*
Monitors the inbox, auto-sorts incoming mail, drafts and sends replies in the brand's voice. Prioritises urgent emails for human review.
**Target clients:** Any business receiving high email volume.

### 4.5 No-show Follow-up Agent *(Retention)*
Detects missed appointments and automatically sends friendly SMS/email follow-ups with rebooking links. Reduces lost revenue from no-shows.
**Target clients:** Clinics, beauty salons, fitness studios, consultants.

### 4.6 Meeting Booker Agent *(Scheduling)*
Takes inbound meeting requests (via chat, email, or web form) and books confirmed calendar slots — handling timezone differences and cancellations automatically.
**Target clients:** Consultants, agencies, B2B service businesses.

### 4.7 Google Reviews Agent *(Reputation)*
After a completed service, automatically sends personalised outreach (SMS/email) asking the client to leave a Google review. Monitors incoming reviews and drafts AI-written responses for the business owner to approve or auto-post.
**Target clients:** Any business dependent on local search and Google reputation — restaurants, clinics, tradespeople, retail.

### 4.8 Custom Agents *(Any need)*
If a business has a repetitive workflow not covered above, DinDrift will scope, design, and build a tailored agent. The limit is the client's imagination.

## 9. Operations

### Delivery Process
1. **Discovery call** (30 min) — understand the business, identify highest-value automation
2. **Proposal** — scoped deliverables, timeline, price
3. **Build** (1–2 weeks) — agent development, integration, testing
4. **Handover** — walkthrough, documentation, training
5. **Maintenance** — monthly check-in, performance review, improvements

### Tools & Stack
- **AI models:** Claude (Anthropic), GPT-4, Gemini (model chosen per use-case)
- **Automation:** n8n, Make (Integromat), custom Next.js API routes
- **Integrations:** Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, E-conomic, Billy, Podio, Twilio (SMS), EmailJS
- **CRM/tracking:** Notion (internal), Linear (issues)
- **Hosting:** Vercel (client sites + agents), Railway (backend services)

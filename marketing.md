## Go-to-Market Phases

### Phase 1 — Credibility (Months 1–3)
- Launch DinDrift website ✅
- Build 2–3 case studies / demo agents (pro-bono or self-funded)
- LinkedIn content: demos, results, behind-the-scenes builds
- Direct outreach to 5–10 target businesses in Copenhagen

### Phase 2 — First Clients (Months 3–6)
- Free 30-minute discovery call offer
- Pilot project at reduced rate in exchange for testimonial + case study
- Referral programme: DKK 1,000 credit per converted referral

### Phase 3 — Scale (Months 6–18)
- Systemise delivery with templated agent frameworks per vertical
- Partner with accountants, web designers, and business coaches
- Consider white-label offering for agencies
